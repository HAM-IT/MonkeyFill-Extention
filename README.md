# 🐒 MonkeyFill (by HABOMIC)

**Your Honest Career Coach — AI-Powered Job Application Assistant**

MonkeyFill analyzes job postings, scores your match, generates tailored CVs, and autofills applications — all from your browser.

---

## 🚀 Installation

1. Download and unzip the MonkeyFill folder
2. Open Chrome and go to `chrome://extensions`
3. Enable **Developer mode** (toggle in top-right)
4. Click **"Load unpacked"** and select the MonkeyFill folder
5. Pin the 🐒 MonkeyFill icon to your toolbar

---

## 📄 Getting Started

1. Click the MonkeyFill icon → **Settings ⚙️**
2. Upload your **master CV** (PDF format)
3. MonkeyFill will extract your professional profile securely
4. Go to any job posting and click the MonkeyFill icon to start!

---

## ✨ Features

### 🧠 Job Analysis (1 Credit)
Paste a job description or analyze the current page. MonkeyFill will:
- Score your match probability (0–100%)
- Identify matched & missing skills
- Give honest career coaching advice
- Suggest a personalized growth roadmap
- Recommend a creative stand-out move

### 📄 Tailored CV
Generates a job-specific CV from your profile:
- Skills reordered by relevance
- Experience bullets reframed for the target role
- Professional summary written for this specific job
- Download as a clean, print-ready PDF

### 🎨 CV Themes & Layouts
Choose from 5 color themes and 3 layout templates in Settings.

### 🌍 Multi-Language
Generate CVs in English or French.

## 🔒 Privacy & Security

- Your CV is processed **locally in your browser** using PDF.js
- AI analysis is powered by secure cloud models (Groq & Gemini)
- Your data is stored in your browser's local storage — never on third-party servers
- Anonymized profile metadata may be stored to improve our services
- We **never sell** your personal data
- No tracking, no ads
